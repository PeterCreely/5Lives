WELCOME TO

Welcome to 5Lives Flip. Choose solo or multiplayer. Then choose your difficulty. Hard is 5 seconds per round, medium is 10 seconds and easy is 15 seconds. When you are ready click the ready button. The game will count down from 3 and then flip the cards to reveal your letters. You will then have either 5, 10 or 15 seconds to review the letters. The cards will then flip back and you can then select your word (up to 8 letters). Once you have picked your word you click submit and the word will be checked and points will be given with each letter a value depending on the difficulty of the letter. If you are playing two player, pass the game to them to let them have their turn. There are five turns per game to see who can get the highest score. Good Luck!
