Welcome to 5Lives!
This is the English version.
There are 5 words ranging from 3 letters to 7 and you have 5 guesses to solve them all. Pick at letter and if it is in one of the 5 words it will appear in those words. If not you will lose one of you 5 guesses. If you use all 5 guesses without solving all words you will lose a life.
You have only 5 Lives!
If you solve all the words you will move to the next level which has only 4 words, 4, 5, 6 & 7 letters each. This continues until there is only 1 seven letter word to solve and then it's back to 5. You start with 5 points and every level earns you another point. Every life lost losses you a point.
What high score can you get?
Good Luck!
